How To Update Device Firmware?

This topic provides step-by-step instructions for safely updating the firmware of your IoT device, including tips to avoid interruptions and a reference to troubleshooting resources.

Warning:
Ensure that your device remains powered on and connected to a stable network during the entire update process. Interruptions may result in device malfunction or failed installation.

STEPS

  1. Open the manufacturer’s app or access the device’s web interface.
  2. Navigate to the “Settings” or “Device Info” section to check the current firmware version.
  3. If an update is available, tap “Update Firmware” or follow the link to the manufacturer’s website.
  4. Download the latest firmware file through the app or from the official support page.
  5. Start the update process and follow the on-screen instructions.
  6. Do not turn off the device or close the app during the update.
  7. Once the update completes, verify the firmware version again to confirm success.

If the update fails or causes issues, refer to Troubleshoot Firmware Issues.
